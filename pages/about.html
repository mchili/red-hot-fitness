About Red Hot Fitness

Red Hot Fitness was created in 2015 by me, Maddie Chili. I'm an Elon Univeristy student who loves everything and anything involving healthy living. I grew up in the world of sports, particularly basketball, volleyball and soccer. Healthy living has been a big part of my life, thanks to my wonderful family. When I arrived at college my love for fitness and nutrition only grew. I am a fitness instructor at Zenitry and Burlington Krav Maga and I love every second of it.

I made this site with the Elon student in mind, but I hope it applies to all college students. My wild curiosity and thirst for knowledge makes me search for the lastest fitness trends and best nutrition tips. This website is my platform to share all of my findings and experiences with you. I want to inspire you to live happy, smart and healthly lives without the stress. I hope to give you the tools to create the lifestyle you want.

It's easy to think there is no time to get in shape, eat healthier or change your lifestyle for the better. It's your time to shine with the help of Red Hot Fitness.
