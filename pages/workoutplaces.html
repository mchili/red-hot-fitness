Best Places to Workout at Elon

Tired of Elon’s Campus Recreation? Discover some hidden gems outside of the Elon bubble. All of these places are within a 5-mile radius, so they won’t drain your gas bill. Plus if you want to get an extra workout in you can bike to these places. Try one out to spice up your workout and leave you coming back for more!

Burlington Krav Maga

Missing some intensity in your workout? Discover your inner warrior at this Krav Maga and fitness studio. Whether you are looking to whip yourself into great shape, learn valuable self defense skills or train in a program that makes you feel powerful and confident, this studio has it all.

Burlington Krav Maga offers two membership options, fitness and warrior membership. A fitness membership features access to all the heavy bag kickboxing classes and other fitness classes such as Kettlebell. Grab your gloves and get ready to release some stress and lots of sweat. If you don’t have a pair of boxing gloves you can borrow some from the studio, purchase them through the studio, wrap your hands or go hard-core and bare hand it. The warrior membership features access to the self-defense and fitness classes. Self-defense classes include all the Krav Maga level classes, Sparring Skills, Ground Defense and Sparology. The self-defense classes offer training for Krav Maga, a hand-to-hand combat system developed by the Israeli Defense Forces. Not only will Krav Maga get you into fantastic shape, it will also give you knowledge about “real world” self defense.

This studio is high quality. Burlington Krav Maga is led by 1 of 7 female Black Belt Instructors in the USA, Shannon Marshall, who is also the owner. It is also Burlington’s only Fit to Fight Training Center. Can’t wait to start? Try your first class for free by signing up online.

Zenitry

Looking for some zen in your chaotic life? Come visit Zenitry. Zenitry is a mind/body balance studio that offers a wide variety of classes such as Yoga, Pilates, Barre and Tabata. A full list and description of each class can be found on Zenitry’s website. These classes will allow you reach your fitness and mental health goals to get a better overall health experience.

Zenitry is also a Phoenix Card vendor that offers membership discounts for students. You can pay per class, buy a set of classes or purchase monthly memberships. To find out more about membership prices call Zenitry or stop in. Your first class is also free at this studio, you can sign up online or walk into the class of your choice.

Another unique aspect of Zenitry is the ”Zen Zone”. Zen Zone gives members an opportunity to come in during the afternoon when no classes are scheduled and hang out at Zenitry. The space is completely open with couches and chairs. It also offers free water, coffee and tea for members though donations are always welcomed. Free wifi is also included. There is also a meditation room making Zenitry a great study and hangout place for Elon students. Students can also melt their stress away by making an appointment with the studio’s massage therapist on-hand. Zenitry has everything Elon students need to relax, work out some stress, and reach their fitness and health goals.

Jerry and Jeanne Robertson Track and Field Complex

If you are looking for a free place to up your workout game, go to Elon’s own track. The track is usually open 24-7 and it is one of Elon’s hidden gems for workout fanatics. Not only will the track make you feel like a professional athlete sprinting around the track, it also introduces your body to a new surface and way to workout. If you don’t know how to get the best workout out of the track check out “A Runner’s Secret Workout”.
